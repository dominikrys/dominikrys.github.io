Executing Stream Cipher Attacks on IP Packets

For part of my bachelor’s dissertation, I implemented and executed a bit-flipping attack on encrypted IP packets in LTE networks. The attack was first documented by David Rupprecht et al. in their academic paper “Breaking LTE on Layer Two”. The attack is possible due to a specification flaw in LTE standards, where IP packets are not integrity protected.
